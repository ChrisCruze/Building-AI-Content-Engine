Cuculi AI Content Engine

The Agent Force: A specialized, modular multi-agent system designed for scalable, goal-oriented content generation.

Unlike monolithic models, the Cuculi engine deploys specialized agents across three functional categories—Core, Strategic, and Specialized—allowing for dynamic workflow orchestration based on specific business goals.
1. Core Workflow Agents
The foundational pipeline for every asset.
The Strategist
Plans funnel stages & psychological triggers.
Signal Hunter
Gathers MongoDB data & external research.
The Architect
Structures flow, outlines, and logic.
The Wordsmith
Generates brand-aligned first drafts.
The Refiner & Judge
Polishes copy and enforces quality control.
2. Strategic Agents
Unique angles for timing and virality.
The Provocateur
Creates high-arousal, controversial hooks.
Seasonal Strategist
Aligns content with holidays & trends.
The Event Hunter
Connects blogs to real upcoming events.
Sticky Marketer
Applies 'Made-to-Stick' SUCCESs framework.
3. Specialized Agents
Enhancements for tone and engagement.
Headline Master
Optimizes titles for curiosity gaps/clicks.
The Storyteller
Structures content into narrative arcs.
The Data Weaver
Integrates statistics naturally into text.
Empathy Engine
Validates user fears to build trust.
