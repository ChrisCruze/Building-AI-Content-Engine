# The Essential Documentation Stack for an AI-Powered Content Engine

## Building Smart Systems for Social Dining Growth

> "Documents that define **intent and direction** come before documents that define **technology**."

- **Strategy before systems**: Clarify your content goals and audience before building AI tools[^1].
- **Funnel-driven content mapping**: Match content types to user journey stages and platforms[^2].
- **Research powers relevance**: AI quality depends on timely, curated signals from the real world[^3].
- **Architecture follows purpose**: Systems should scale based on clearly defined strategic needs[^4].
- **Prompting is last, not first**: Define AI agent roles only after strategy, funnel, and research are in place[^5].
