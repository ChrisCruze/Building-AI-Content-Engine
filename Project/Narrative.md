# Narrative

## What this is
A living explanation of how this project evolved.
This captures reasoning, learning, and changes in perspective.

## How to use
- Write in paragraphs
- Explain *why* decisions were made
- Capture tradeoffs, reversals, and insights
- Update as your understanding changes

## Why this exists
Future-you should understand not just what happened, but why.
This reduces rethinking, doubt, and decision fatigue.

## How to update
- Revise, don't just append
- Reflect when something changes
- Keep it human and honest
