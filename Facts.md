# Facts

> Canonical decisions and constraints for Building AI Content Engine.

## Platform

**Platform Name**: Cuculi  
**Platform Type**: Social dining platform  
**Core Value Proposition**: Connecting food lovers and social connectors through shared dining experiences in a low-pressure, community-focused environment.

## Target Audience Segments

### 1. Social Connectors
- **Motivation**: Seeking meaningful connections through shared dining experiences
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty finding like-minded people, awkward first meetings
- **Content Needs**: Stories of successful connections, tips for social dining, community highlights

### 2. Food Enthusiasts
- **Motivation**: Passion for culinary experiences and discovering new restaurants
- **Pain Points**: Wanting to try new places but lacking dining companions
- **Content Needs**: Restaurant reviews, food trends, culinary event coverage

### 3. Urban Professionals
- **Motivation**: Networking and building relationships in a social context
- **Pain Points**: Limited time, need for efficient social activities
- **Content Needs**: Quick guides, event summaries, professional networking tips

### 4. New City Residents
- **Motivation**: Building a social circle in a new location
- **Pain Points**: Loneliness, unfamiliarity with local scene
- **Content Needs**: City guides, local event highlights, community building content

## Content Types

1. **Blog Articles**
   - Length: 800-2000 words
   - Frequency: 2-4 articles per week
   - Format: Markdown with frontmatter

2. **Social Media Posts**
   - Platforms: Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok
   - Frequency: 1-3 posts per day per platform
   - Formats: Text, image captions, video scripts

3. **Newsletter Content**
   - Length: 500-1500 words total
   - Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
   - Format: HTML email template, plain text alternative

## Key Stakeholders

- **Platform Users**: Target audience for content
- **CEO**: Strategic alignment and positioning
- **Content Team**: Execution and content creation
- **Growth Marketer/Head of Marketing**: Strategy and audience insights (Accountable Owner for Growth Strategy)
- **Product Manager**: User insight and platform positioning
- **Engineering Team**: Technical implementation and system architecture

## Technology Stack

- **LLM Tools**: ChatGPT, OpenAI, Claude
- **Automation**: N8n for workflow orchestration
- **Research Sources**: News APIs, social media APIs, platform analytics, user feedback systems, content performance data

## Success Metrics and KPIs

### Awareness Metrics
- Content reach and impressions
- Brand mention volume
- Search visibility for platform-related terms

### Engagement Metrics
- Click-through rates from content to platform
- Time spent on content
- Social shares and comments

### Conversion Metrics
- Sign-up rate from content traffic
- Profile completion rate
- First event attendance rate

### Quality Metrics
- Content engagement quality (meaningful comments vs. spam)
- Brand sentiment in responses
- Content relevance scores

### Target Benchmarks
- **Blog Articles**: 5%+ click-through to sign-up page, 3+ minute average read time
- **Social Posts**: 5%+ engagement rate, 2%+ click-through to platform
- **Newsletters**: 25%+ open rate, 5%+ click-through rate

## Brand Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Warm and Inviting**: Friendly, approachable, welcoming
- **Authentic**: Genuine, honest, not overly polished
- **Community-Focused**: Emphasizes connection and belonging
- **Food-Positive**: Celebrates culinary experiences without being pretentious
- **Inclusive**: Welcoming to all backgrounds, dietary preferences, and comfort levels

### Tone Guidelines by Content Type
- **Blog Content**: Informative but conversational, clear, helpful, engaging
- **Social Media**: Casual, friendly, engaging, short, visual, shareable
- **Newsletters**: Personal, community-oriented, story-driven, event-focused

## Content Funnel Stages

1. **Awareness Stage**: Introduce platform to new audiences
2. **Consideration Stage**: Help users understand value and reduce friction
3. **Decision Stage**: Motivate sign-up and first event attendance
4. **Activation Stage**: Ensure successful first experience
5. **Retention Stage**: Keep users engaged between events

## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Quality Standards
- **Grammar and Spelling**: Zero errors (automated checking required)
- **Brand Voice Compliance**: Must pass brand voice scoring (80%+ match)
- **Factual Accuracy**: All claims must be verifiable or clearly marked as opinion
- **Originality**: Content must pass plagiarism detection (95%+ original)
- **Readability**: Flesch-Kincaid score appropriate for target audience (60-70)

### Content Scoring Criteria
- **Relevance**: Content aligns with strategic goals (scored 1-10)
- **Engagement Potential**: Likely to drive desired user actions (scored 1-10)
- **Brand Alignment**: Matches voice, tone, and positioning (scored 1-10)
- **Technical Quality**: Grammar, structure, formatting (scored 1-10)

**Minimum Threshold**: Average score of 7.0 across all criteria

## Platform Positioning

**Positioning Statement**: "The platform where food lovers and social connectors meet over shared dining experiences, creating meaningful connections in a low-pressure, community-focused environment."

**Differentiation**:
- **vs. Dating Apps**: Less pressure, more social, food-focused
- **vs. Food Apps**: Social-first, connection-oriented, not just reviews
- **vs. Event Platforms**: Curated dining experiences, community-focused, relationship-building

## Content Guardrails

### Must Always
- Reflect platform values of inclusivity and community
- Provide genuine value to readers
- Maintain brand consistency
- Include clear calls-to-action when appropriate
- Respect user privacy and data

### Must Never
- Make false claims or promises
- Use discriminatory language or imagery
- Violate user privacy
- Create content that could harm platform reputation
- Generate content without human review for sensitive topics

### Sensitive Topics Requiring Human Review
- Safety and security content
- Policy changes or legal matters
- Crisis communications
- Controversial topics
- User-generated content featuring real people
