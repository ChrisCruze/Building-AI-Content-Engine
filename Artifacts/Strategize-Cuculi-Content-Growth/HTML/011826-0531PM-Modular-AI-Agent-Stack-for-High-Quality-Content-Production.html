3. Writer
The Storyteller
Primary Goal

Strategic Value
1. Originality: By forcing the Researcher to find specific, non-obvious insights before writing begins, the system prevents the "average" hallucinations common in single-shot prompts.
2. Scalability: The modular nature means you can swap the "Voice" document to repurpose the same Research/Outline for different channels (e.g., Blog vs. Newsletter) instantly.
